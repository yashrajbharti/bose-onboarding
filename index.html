Make sure Bluetooth functionality is turned on or enabled on your source device. Your Bluetooth device may be out of range—try moving it closer to the speaker. If possible, turn off all other nearby Bluetooth devices or disable their Bluetooth functionality. Reset the speaker. Press the Mute button for ten seconds, until the indicators briefly flash. OPERATION

The control buttons are located on the top of the speaker.

Power Button: Press to turn the speaker on or off. Press and hold to check the battery charge state.

Mute Button: Press to mute/unmute sound from the speaker.

Volume (-) Button: Minus (-) decreases speaker volume. Press and hold the button to continuosly decrease the volume.

Volume (+) Button: Plus (+) increases speaker volume. Press and hold the button to continuously increase the volume.

Bluetooth Button: Press to select the Bluetooth source. The speaker attempts to connect to the last paired device. If there are no paired devices, the speaker goes into discoverable mode.

AUX Button: Press to select the source connected to the AUX input.

REPLACING THE BATTERY

The battery should be replaced when:
